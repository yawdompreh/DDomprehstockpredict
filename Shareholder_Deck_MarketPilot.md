## Slide 3 - Problem We Solve
Retail investors and small advisory firms face three gaps:
- Data fragmentation across price, fundamentals, and news sentiment
- Analysis complexity requiring advanced tools and technical skill
- Slow decision cycles due to manual comparison of multiple tickers

Result:
- Missed opportunities
- Inconsistent decision quality
- Lower confidence during volatile markets

---

## Slide 4 - Our Solution
MarketPilot AI delivers:
- Unified analysis pipeline for price behavior, financial quality, and sentiment
- AI-generated Buy, Hold, or Sell recommendation per ticker
- Confidence scoring and explanatory report output
- Visual charting for trend interpretation
- Downloadable institutional-style reports for audit and collaboration

## Slide 6 - AI and Data Advantage
Model stack:
- Technical analysis features: returns, volatility, RSI, MACD, SMA bands
- Fundamentals score: growth, profitability, leverage, valuation signals
- Sentiment score: headline polarity from financial RSS feeds
- Ensemble decision layer: weighted composite score to recommendation class

Why this matters:
- Balanced signal design reduces overreliance on one market dimension
- Explainable output improves investor trust and adoption
